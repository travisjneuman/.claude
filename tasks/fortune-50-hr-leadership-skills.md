## Research Findings

### Talent Acquisition
- Skills-based hiring expands talent pool by 2.6x (Indeed)
- Only 8% of Fortune 100 cybersecurity roles offer remote flexibility
- Internal mobility 2.5x more predictive of retention than promotions
- AARRR Framework: Acquisition, Activation, Retention, Referral, Renewal

### Performance Management
- OKRs increasingly adopted alongside competency frameworks
- Continuous performance management replacing annual reviews
- ESG metrics now in 73% of S&P 500 executive compensation

### Compensation Strategy
- Stock awards >50% of S&P 500 CEO total compensation
- Total Rewards Philosophy includes base, short-term incentives, long-term incentives, benefits
- Pay equity and transparency becoming critical

### Change Management
- ADKAR: Awareness, Desire, Knowledge, Ability, Reinforcement
- Kotter's 8-Step Model for large-scale transformation
- McKinsey 7-S Framework for strategic alignment
- 70% transformation failure rate despite documented methodologies

### Crisis Management
- 90% of CFOs use 3+ scenarios for planning
- Scenario planning critical for geopolitical risk
- Fortune 500 boards hiring Chief Geopolitical Officers

### Board Relations
- 55% of directors believe at least one colleague should be replaced
- 44% of S&P 500 companies have CEO as board chair
- Trust foundation critical between CEO and board
- NACD framework emphasizes ongoing communication beyond formal meetings

### Stakeholder Management
- Business Roundtable redefined corporate purpose to include all stakeholders
- Only 12% of CEOs describe themselves as successful with regulators
- 33% of corporate earnings influenced by regulatory engagement
